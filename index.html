Problem Statement 📂 Open Word Doc

Context: The food delivery industry is becoming increasingly competitive, with businesses facing challenges in understanding customer behaviour, optimizing delivery performance, and maximizing revenue. Despite having large volumes of data, it is often difficult to identify key factors that influence order demand, customer spending patterns, and service efficiency.

Our Solution: This project addresses the problem by analysing food ordering data using Tableau to uncover meaningful patterns and trends. The dataset includes key attributes such as Order ID, Order Value, Delivery Fee, Cuisine, Meal Type, City, and Time Taken for Delivery.

Proposed Solution 📂 Open Word Doc

A web analytics hub integrating 9 subtask dashboards with localized notes workspaces to allow Business Analysts and Operations Managers to collaborate in real-time on visual reports.

Solution Architecture 📂 Open Word Doc

Multi-layered model comprising data storage (CSV), analytics visualization engine (Tableau Server), web hosting container (index.html), and secure browser caching layer (localStorage).

Preprocessing & Visual Subtasks 📄 Open PDF Document

Summary of the 9 visual subtasks designed in Tableau:

  1. KPI Demonstration: Evaluating order counts, revenues, and global ratings.
  2. Flavors Across Cities: Heatmap checking regional cuisine demands.
  3. Order Distribution by Demos: Stacked bar checking restaurant selections.
  4. Distribution of Cuisines: Pie chart illustrating overall share.
  5. Orders by Company: Solo consumption spikes across meal slots.
  6. Donut Ratings: Average rating breakdown per slot.
  7. Top 5 Cities: Mumbai leading over Pune and Bangalore.
  8. Revenues: Breakfast/Snacks contributing top revenue metrics.
  9. Delivery Times: Most shipments completing within 4-10 minute slots.
